Who I Am

Cloud Journey

Hey! I'm Pragna — a cloud and observability engineer focused on building infrastructure that quietly does its job. I work from the principle that great infrastructure should be invisible until something goes wrong — and even then, it should tell you exactly what happened.

I work across cloud platforms (AWS being my primary focus), containerization with Docker and Kubernetes, and the observability stack — Prometheus, Grafana, and distributed tracing. My focus is shipping systems that are not just functional, but observable and resilient.
